Brand Identity Guidelines

ETHICS METAL FORMING MACHINERIES — INDIA

1Brand Overview

ETHICS Metal Forming Machineries India is positioned as an engineering-grade industrial brand — precision, strength, and reliability expressed visually through a brushed-metal wordmark, a deep structural navy, and disciplined steel-gray supporting type. This document defines how the mark and its colors should be used consistently across stationery, signage, brochures, exhibition stands, packaging, and digital platforms.

Brand essence: Engineering precision · Industrial trust · Manufacturing strength
India · Metal Forming Machinery

2Logo

The primary logo consists of three locked elements: the metallic "ETHICS" wordmark, the framed "METAL FORMING MACHINERIES" descriptor bar, and the "INDIA" sub-mark. These three elements must always appear together in the same proportions — never separated or rearranged.

Primary — on white / light backgrounds
On dark navy / photographic backgrounds

Clear Space & Minimum Size

Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "I" in ETHICS (marked as X below). No text, imagery, or graphic element should enter this zone. Do not scale the logo below 30mm wide in print or 160px wide on screen — the framed descriptor bar becomes illegible below this size.

Incorrect Usage

The integrity of the metallic gradient and the locked lockup must never be compromised. Avoid the following:

✕ Don't skew
✕ Don't stretch
✕ Don't recolor

Also avoid: placing the logo on busy/low-contrast photography without a solid backing panel, separating "ETHICS" from the "METAL FORMING MACHINERIES INDIA" descriptor, adding drop shadows or outlines, or reproducing it below minimum size.

4Typography

The logo uses a custom, heavily-engineered italic display face that should not be reproduced for body copy. For all collateral, pair it with clean, modern sans-serifs that echo its precision without competing with it.

Display / Headlines
Montserrat ExtraBold Italic
Sub-heads / Labels
Segoe UI Semibold — Tracked Caps
Body Copy
Segoe UI / Calibri Regular — for brochures, specs sheets, and correspondence. Set in Navy Deep or 80% black for print legibility.

5Voice & Positioning

Tone

Confident, technical, and precise. Communications should sound like an engineer talking to an engineer — direct claims, real specifications, no exaggerated marketing language.

Keywords

Precision · Durability · Performance · Indian Manufacturing · Heavy Engineering · Reliability · Forming Technology

6Application Reference

StationeryLogo top-left or centered on letterhead, Navy Deep rule beneath header, Steel Mid contact bar in footer.
Exhibition / SignageLogo on Navy Background panel (reversed lockup), generous clear space, steel-frame border details echoing the logo's bar.
Digital / WebsiteNavy Deep as primary UI color, Steel grays for backgrounds/dividers, white space kept generous to let the metallic mark read clearly.
Vehicle / Machine LiveryLogo at minimum 300mm width, full color only — no single-color or reversed-only application on equipment.
Documents (quotes, datasheets)Logo top-right, Gunmetal Text (#717170) for body copy, Navy Mid for section headers.
